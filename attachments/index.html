About

The Redirectory Project is a free and open project built by Randall Leeds, Max Ogden and Brian Rice and endorsed by Code for America to create a detailed directory of social services that anyone can contribute to or use.

Mission

Many cities have existing social services that aren't easily searchable by hours of operation, location and available resources. You can help out by adding to our directory.

All information collected by the Redirectory Project is public domain and can be used by anyone. See the Developers page for more information on how to access the data.

Getting Involved

Getting involved is easy – first check and see if your city is participating by viewing our list of active cities.

If your city is listed you can tell us about services in your area so that we can add them to the directory. If your city isn't yet active you can learn how to get your city listed in Redirectory
